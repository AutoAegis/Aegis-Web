The Bloodline

Aegis is a unified bloodline operating across Roblox and Discord. It is defined by hierarchy, discipline, and authority. Rank is not cosmetic; it represents responsibility, legacy, and power within the lineage.

The Hierarchy

Autarch

Supreme authority of Aegis. Absolute command over the bloodline.

Regent

Executor of the Autarch’s will and overseer of all domains.

Archduke

High sovereign entrusted with large-scale governance and strategy.

Duke

Regional authority maintaining order and influence.

Lord

Noble commander responsible for loyalty and discipline.

Cardinal

Keeper of doctrine, tradition, and internal integrity.

Marquess

Guardian of borders and external relations.

Marchioness

Strategic noble overseeing coordinated operations.

Viscount

Officer of command supporting higher nobility.

Viscountess

Operational leader trusted with execution of directives.

Envoy

Official representative of Aegis to outsiders.

Consul

Advisor responsible for counsel and policy.

Archon

Administrator maintaining order within the structure.

The Aegis Council

Collective body guiding the bloodline’s long-term future.

Maestro

Master of training, refinement, and discipline.

Warden

Enforcer of rules and protector of internal stability.

Nobles

Established members entrusted with leadership potential.

Knights

Active operatives sworn to defend the bloodline.

Proletariats

Foundation of the bloodline, bound by loyalty and service.

Allies

External forces aligned with Aegis interests.

Heretics

Those who oppose or betray the bloodline.

Supporters

Observers and sympathizers without rank or authority.

Unity Through Order

Every rank serves a purpose. Every member bears weight. The strength of Aegis lies in structure.
